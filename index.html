Documentation for the open source fonts Miao Unicode & Mro Unicode

Miao Unicode

Supports the Miao (Pollard) script:

  • Invented around 1905.
  • Used by several language groups in Yunnan Province (southern China) and surrounding areas.

Sample of the Miao (Pollard) script [source]

Mro Unicode

Supports the Mro script:

  • Invented in the 1980s.
  • Used by the Mro (or “Mru”) people of the Chittagong Hill Tracts, southeast Bangladesh.
